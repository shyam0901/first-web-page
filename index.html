Skin Care
Routine Steps

Here's a simple and effective daily skincare routine, with both morning and night steps. You can customize it based on your skin type (oily, dry, combination, sensitive, acne-prone).

Join Now

🌞Morning Skincare Routine Cleanser 1.Gently wash your face to remove oil and sweat from the night. → Use a mild foaming or gel cleanser. 2.Toner (optional) Balances your skin's pH and preps it for other products. → Use alcohol-free toner if you have sensitive or dry skin. 3.Serum Targets specific concerns (e.g., Vitamin C for brightening, Hyaluronic Acid for hydration). → Apply while skin is slightly damp. 4.Moisturizer Keeps skin hydrated and seals in the serum. → Choose lightweight gel for oily skin, cream for dry skin. 5.Sunscreen (SPF 30 or higher) The most important step to protect from sun damage and premature aging. → Use daily, even if indoors. 🌙 Night Skincare Routine Makeup Remover / Oil Cleanser (if wearing makeup or sunscreen) Breaks down makeup and oil-based products. 1.Cleanser Wash off the day's dirt and impurities. 2.Exfoliation (2-3 times a week only) Removes dead skin cells for smoother texture. → Choose a gentle chemical exfoliant (like AHA/BHA) instead of scrubs. 3.Toner (optional) As in the morning. 4.Treatment / Serum More targeted treatments like: 5.Retinol (for anti-aging/acne) 6.Niacinamide (for pores and texture) 7.Salicylic acid (for acne) 8.Eye Cream (optional) For dark circles, puffiness, or fine lines. 9.Moisturizer / Night Cream Nourishes and locks in moisture overnight. 10.🧖‍♂️ Weekly Add-ons Face Mask: Hydrating, clay, or sheet masks (1-2x/week depending on needs) Facial Massage or Gua Sha: Improves circulation and reduces puffiness
